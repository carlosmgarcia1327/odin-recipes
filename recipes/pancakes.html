Pancakes

Description

This recipe will take about 30 minutes and yield 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 3/4 cups sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large eggs
    • <1i>1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon butter, softened, or as needed, for greasing, plus more for serving.
  • toppings of choice, e.g. maple syrup or fruit, for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat a griddle to 300 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Stir together milk and vinegar in a small bowl or a 4-cup liquid measuring cup.
  3. Whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a second bowl.
  4. Beat eggs in a large bowl until smooth. Add vanilla and milk texture; whisk together. Add flour mixture to bowl with egg mixture and whisk until just combined. (Batter will be lumpy). Let rest for 3 minutes and gently stir again.
  5. Melt some butter on the griddle, then carefully wipe with a paper towel. (You want just a bit of sheen; don't overgrease the griddle). Sppon batter in 1/3-cup portions onto griddle. Cook until bibbles form on surface of batter, about 2 1/2 minutes.
  6. Flip pancakes and cook until browned on the other side, about 1 1/2 minutes more. Repeat with remaining batter. Serve immediately with butter and toppings.
